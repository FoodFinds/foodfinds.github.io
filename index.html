What we do

At FoodFinds we envision a world in which no food is wasted. We seek to address food waste and food insecurity in a feel-good way by providing a smooth and enjoyable user experience.

1:1 Meetups

Share and get food by contacting FoodFinds users. Your next yummy food that would have been wasted awaits you a post reply away!

Meal Swipe Exchanges

Have too many weekly swipes and it's already Sunday? Or maybe you've had one of those busy finals weeks in which you can't get enough JJs'. We've got you covered! FoodFinds will match you with the closest available swipe!

Club Events

Use the map view or the list view to locate the closest available leftover food from club events. No more cookies are going to waste.

Pantries

Always know what's in the closest pantry to you. You can drop off or claim items in pantries.
